Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how fastfol.io ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website, https://fastfol.io (the "Website"). This policy will help you understand what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and collect information for various purposes, including website analytics and marketing.

Types of Cookies We Use

  1. Essential Cookies

    These cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly. They enable you to navigate the Website and use its essential features, such as accessing secure areas.

  2. Analytical/Performance Cookies

    We use analytical cookies, such as Google Analytics, to collect information about how visitors use our Website. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Website, what pages they visit, and if they encounter any errors. The information collected is anonymous and does not identify individual users. We use this data to improve the performance and user experience of our Website.

  3. Functionality Cookies

    Functionality cookies allow the Website to remember choices you make, such as your language preferences and customization settings. These cookies enhance your experience by providing personalized features.

  4. Targeting/Advertising Cookies

    We may use third-party advertising cookies to display relevant advertisements to you on our Website or on other websites you visit. These cookies are used to track your browsing habits and deliver advertisements that are tailored to your interests.

Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences by adjusting the settings in your web browser. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Please note that if you choose to disable cookies, some features of the Website may not function properly, and your browsing experience may be impacted.

Third-Party Cookies

Please be aware that third-party services, such as Google Analytics, may place cookies on our Website for tracking and analytical purposes. These cookies are subject to the privacy policies of the respective third parties. We do not have control over the cookies placed by these third parties.

Protecting Your Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The information collected through cookies is used in accordance with our Privacy Policy. For more information on how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information, please review our Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Cookie Policy or your cookie preferences, please contact us at hello@fastfol.io.

Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date at the top of the policy will indicate the last update. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.
