Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how fastfol.io ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your personal information when you visit our website, https://fastfol.io (the "Website"). This policy outlines our commitment to your privacy and your rights concerning your personal data.

Sharing Your Information

  2. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, or protect our rights and the rights of others.

Your Privacy Choices

  2. Cookie Preferences: You can manage your cookie preferences as described in our Cookie Policy.
